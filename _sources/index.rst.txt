Welcome to MPHYFEM's documentation!
===================================

Hello! Welcome to the FEM solvers for Multiphysics problems in Solid Mechanics.

The finite element formulation is published in the following papers in

* `Mixed formulation <https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/15376494.2020.1762952>`_
* `Electro-Mechanics  <https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0045782520306289>`_
* `Magneto-Mechanics <https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0167663621004142>`_
* `Morphoelasticity (Growth) <https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0022509620304919>`_
